⏱ SyncFast — Intermittent Fasting Tracker

A simple, beautiful, and 100% offline intermittent fasting tracker. No sign-up. No server. Just you and your fast.

Show Image

✨ Features

⏱ Fasting Timer — Start and stop your fast with a live countdown ring showing your progress
📊 Dashboard — View your total fasts, day streak, average fast duration, and weekly charts
📅 History — Browse a monthly calendar showing completed, partial, and missed fasting days
📝 Journal — Log your mood, energy level, and notes for each fasting day
🎯 Goals — Track progress toward weekly fasts, monthly hours, weight goals, and streak targets
⚖️ Weight Tracking — Log your weight in kg or lbs and visualize your trend over time
📤 Export Data — Download your fasting history as CSV or a progress report as PDF
🔥 Streak Tracking — Stay motivated with a live day streak counter
📲 PWA Ready — Install on your phone and use it like a native app, fully offline

🚀 How to Use
Starting a Fast

Open the Timer tab ⏱
Select your fasting plan (16:8, 18:6, OMAD, or Custom)
Tap "Start Fast" — the ring timer begins counting
When done, tap "End Fast" to log your session

Logging Your Weight

On the Timer tab, scroll down to Weight Tracking
Enter your weight and select kg or lbs
Tap "Log" to save it

Writing a Journal Entry

Go to the Journal tab 📝
Select your mood emoji and energy level
Write your notes — what you ate, how you felt
Tap "Save entry"

Viewing Your Progress

Head to Dashboard 📊 for charts and stats
Head to History 📅 for a calendar view of your fasting days
Head to Goals 🎯 to track your targets


📲 Install as an App
Android (Chrome)

Open the app in Chrome
Tap the Install banner or go to browser menu → "Add to Home Screen"

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

Open the app in Safari
Tap the Share button (⬆)
Choose "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add

Once installed, SyncFast runs fully offline like a native app!

🛠️ Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — zero frameworks, zero dependencies
Chart.js for weekly fasting and weight trend charts
LocalStorage for on-device data persistence
PWA-ready for home screen installation
